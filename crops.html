Common Crop Diseases

Wheat Rust

Appears as reddish-brown pustules on leaves and stems. Control with fungicides and resistant varieties.

Rice Blast

Diamond-shaped lesions on leaves. Manage with fungicides, resistant varieties, and proper water management.

Potato Blight

Dark lesions on leaves and stems. Prevent with fungicides, crop rotation, and proper spacing.

Pest Management

Aphids

Small sap-sucking insects that damage plants. Control with insecticidal soaps, neem oil, or beneficial insects.

Bollworms

Caterpillars that feed on cotton bolls and other crops. Manage with Bt crops, pheromone traps, and timely spraying.

Locusts

Swarms that can devastate crops. Early detection and coordinated control measures are essential.
