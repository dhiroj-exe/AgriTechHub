My tomato plants have yellow leaves with brown spots. What could be wrong?

This sounds like Early Blight, a common fungal disease in tomatoes. To treat it:

  • Remove infected leaves immediately
  • Apply copper-based fungicide
  • Ensure proper spacing between plants
  • Water at the base to keep foliage dry

Thanks! What's the best organic fungicide I can use?

For organic control of Early Blight, you can use:

  • Copper soap (approved for organic farming)
  • Neem oil spray (apply every 7-14 days)
  • Bacillus subtilis products
  • Compost tea as a preventative measure
